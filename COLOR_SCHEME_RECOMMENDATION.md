# Classic Professional Color Scheme Recommendation

## For B2B Lead Generation Platform

### Color Philosophy
**Classic, Professional, Clean, Minimal**

### Recommended Palette

#### Light Mode:
- **Primary Blue**: `hsl(217, 91%, 60%)` - Classic navy blue (trust, professionalism, timeless)
- **Primary Dark**: `hsl(217, 91%, 40%)` - Deep navy for hover states
- **Background**: `hsl(0, 0%, 100%)` - Pure white (clean, minimal)
- **Foreground**: `hsl(222, 47%, 11%)` - Near black (high contrast, readable)
- **Secondary**: `hsl(210, 20%, 98%)` - Very light gray (subtle backgrounds)
- **Muted**: `hsl(215, 16%, 50%)` - Medium gray (secondary text)
- **Border**: `hsl(214, 32%, 91%)` - Light gray (subtle dividers)
- **Accent**: `hsl(217, 91%, 60%)` - Same as primary (consistency)

#### Dark Mode:
- **Primary Blue**: `hsl(217, 91%, 65%)` - Lighter navy for visibility
- **Background**: `hsl(222, 47%, 11%)` - Dark navy-gray
- **Foreground**: `hsl(210, 20%, 98%)` - Near white
- **Card**: `hsl(217, 33%, 17%)` - Slightly lighter than background
- **Border**: `hsl(217, 33%, 25%)` - Subtle gray borders

### Why This Palette?

1. **Classic Navy Blue**: Timeless, professional, trustworthy - used by banks, law firms, enterprise software
2. **High Contrast**: Excellent readability (WCAG AAA)
3. **Minimal**: Mostly grayscale with blue accents - no distractions
4. **Professional**: Suitable for B2B sales professionals
5. **Versatile**: Works for both modern and traditional audiences

### Design Principles Applied:
- ✅ Maximum 2-3 colors (blue, white, gray)
- ✅ High contrast for accessibility
- ✅ Clean whitespace
- ✅ Subtle borders and shadows
- ✅ No bright or playful colors
- ✅ Timeless, not trendy
